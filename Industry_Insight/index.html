    Ryan Roslansky

    CEO at LinkedIn 2d ·

    It was an honor to sit down with my next guest on The Path, Chair of Save the Children International, Angela Ahrendts. At first glance, it might be hard to make sense of Angela’s career path. From her earlier jobs in women’s fashion, to CEO of Burberry, then a surprising move to tech as SVP of Retail at Apple, you have to wonder why she chose that meandering route. But when you hear her talk, it’s clear that her passion and need to love whatever she did always took the lead. She made a pact with herself at a young age that she wasn’t going to just have a job. She was going to make sure she did what she loved, and she’s never faltered from that commitment to herself. Even at the jobs she didn’t really want, she worked hard and learned everything she could. She said each job was a warmup for the one that came next. If a job feels like a dead end, look for the opportunities to gain knowledge or learn a new skill that you can take elsewhere. Don’t let the moments be wasted in your career journey.

    Jeyandran Venugopal

    Chief Product and Technology Officer (CPTO) at Flipkart 4d ·

    Just like digital transformation, having a cloud strategy is no longer an option for businesses but a necessity for continued growth. According to @IDC, by 2024, more than 40% of enterprises will use cloud computing to meet increased performance, security, and compliance requirements. As we saw e-commerce grow exponentially, we discovered the power of having a strong cloud strategy in enabling our journey forward. Our cloud strategy is modeled around three parameters: security, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness to organize our infrastructure in the best way possible. This ensures that we can continue to invest in innovation while also delivering the best experience to our customers with a robust digital infrastructure in place. It is essential for businesses, especially with high business volumes like ours, to understand the value that cloud computing technology can help unlock and evaluate the best model for cloud adoption to ensure continued productivity, efficiency, and growth in a secure environment.
